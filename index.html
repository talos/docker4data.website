Large civic datasets. Usable.

Open data is great

But...

  • Portal tools don't do what you want
  • You need to chase down multiple datasets to do real analysis
  • Stitching them back together takes time and research

Meet Docker4Data

Inspired by and leveraging the Docker container ecosystem, Docker4Data can provide the data you need, nicely packaged and ready to use:

  • Relational database prepopulated and ready-to-go
  • A slim front-end with canned queries and data visualization

Up and running in < 10 min

Docker4Data is in alpha right now as we experiment and validate the concept. A single dataset, comprising all of New York City's mortgages and deed transfers going back to 1966, including geographic data.

You can try out a shared instance right now. You can run your own in less than ten minutes.
